Jaipur - Pink City

Hammock Hostels Jaipur

Amer Road energy, fort views in the distance, and deep-work mornings before you disappear into the bazaars.

Workation base on India's heritage spine

Our Jaipur home sits along the Amer Road corridor - minutes from Jorawar Singh Gate and the rhythm of the old city. Start with sunrise walks toward the hills, take calls from bright communal tables, and trade playlists with guests planning Hawa Mahal loops or slow afternoons at Johari Bazaar.

Expect the same Hammock staples: high-speed Wi-Fi, reading and working spaces, self-serve kitchen, laundry, complimentary tea and coffee, and frequent cleaning so long stays stay light.

Tip: confirm exact pin and arrival notes with our team when you book - Jaipur's lanes reward a little local guidance.
